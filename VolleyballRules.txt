1. Six players on a team, three in the front and three in the back.
2. Maximum of three hits per side.
3. The ball may be played off of the net.
4. A ball hitting a boundary line is in.
5. A ball that hits an antennae, floor outside the court, net or cables outside of the antennae, referee stand, or ceiling about nonplayable area is out.
6. It is legal to contact the ball with any part of the body.
7. It is illegal to catch, hold, or throw the ball.
8. A player cannot block or attack a serve from inside the ten foot line.
9. After the serve, players in the front line may switch positions.
